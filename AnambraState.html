Anambra State

Anambra State, a commercial powerhouse in southeastern Nigeria, is home to Onitsha Main Market and a growing industrial sector. With a strong entrepreneurial spirit, key industries span trade, manufacturing, agriculture, and oil & gas. Backed by infrastructure development and economic growth initiatives, it offers prime investment opportunities.

Key Policy Initiatives

  • Ease of doing business reforms
  • Establishment of the Anambra State Investment Promotion and Protection Agency (ANSIPPA) to facilitate investments
  • Encouraging PPPs to drive infrastructure development and economic growth
  • Pioneer status incentive
  • Gas utilisation for companies in gas utilisation downstream
  • Provide aftercare investment protection and support services
  • Facilitate statutory permits, tax permits, waivers, concessions
