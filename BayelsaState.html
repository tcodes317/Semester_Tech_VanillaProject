Key Policy Initiatives

  • Establishment of the Bayelsa State Investment Promotion Agency (BIPA) for ease of doing business
  • Land banking efforts to improve access to farmland for investors
  • Tax incentives for agriculture, aquaculture, and renewable energy sectors
  • Public-Private Partnerships (PPP) to promote infrastructure development
  • Development of one-stop-shop platforms to simplify investment procedures

Competitive Advantage

  • Rich in oil and gas, making it a hub for Nigeria's oil industry
  • Longest coastline in Nigeria (approximately 203km)
  • Extensive agricultural land for large-scale cassava, oil palm, and plantain cultivation
  • Tourism potential with attractions like Lake Efi, Oloibiri Oil Museum, and cultural heritage
  • Presence of research and learning Institutes like NCDMB, Niger Delta University, and Federal University Otuoke
