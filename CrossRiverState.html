CrossRivers State

Cross River State, with Calabar as its capital, is a major hub for tourism, agriculture, and trade, sharing a border with Cameroon. The state is home to attractions like Obudu Mountain Resort and the Calabar Carnival. Its economy thrives on initiatives like the Calabar Free Trade Zone, with ongoing efforts to improve infrastructure and industrial development.
