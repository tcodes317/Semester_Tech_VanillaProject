Osun State

Osun State, in southwestern Nigeria, has a vibrant economy driven by agriculture, commerce, and small-scale industries. Key crops include cocoa, yam, cassava, and oil palm. With growing investment in infrastructure, education, and industrialization, Osun offers solid opportunities for investors.

Key Policy Initiatives

  • Simplified business registration, taxes, land acquisition, and government processes.
  • Encourages private sector involvement with incentives
  • Offers tax breaks, land subsidies, and lowinterest loans. 
  • Supports agriculture and tech industry through hubs and digital skills.  
  • Clear rules for public-private partnerships with fair bidding

Competitive Advantage

  • Osun State has valuable minerals like gold, granite, and talc. 
  • The fertile soil supports crops like cocoa, cassava, and palm oil. 
  • The state has good access to key markets and ports in southwestern Nigeria. 
  • Osun has a well-educated workforce and skilled artisans. 
  • The state is home to UNESCO sites and natural attractions like Olumirin Waterfalls.
