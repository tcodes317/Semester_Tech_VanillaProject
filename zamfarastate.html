Zamfara State

Zamfara State, in northwestern Nigeria, is rich in gold and other solid minerals, making it a key hub for mining. Agriculture also plays a vital role, with crops like millet, sorghum, and maize, alongside livestock farming. The government focuses on peacebuilding, mineral exploration, agriculture, and infrastructure development to drive economic growth.
