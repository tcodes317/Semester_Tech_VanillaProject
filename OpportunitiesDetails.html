Project Overview

Description

The Lagos Tech Innovation Hub will provide co-working spaces, incubation programs, and high-speed infrastructure for startups in fintech, healthtech, and agritech. It aims to attract global VCs, foster job creation, and accelerate Nigeria's digital economy.
