kwara State

Kwara State, situated in north-central Nigeria, is known as the "State of Harmony" for its peaceful coexistence among diverse ethnic groups. It is an emerging industrial and commercial hub, attracting investments in manufacturing and agribusiness. The government prioritizes infrastructure development, agro-industrialization, capacity building, and economic growth.

Key Policy Initiatives

  • Partnered with the National Export Promotion Council (NEPC) to improve Nigerian products for international export standards.
  • The state collaborates with Zenith Bank and FCMB to provide zero and low-interest loans to business owners.

Competitive Advantage

  • The state has rich farmland that supports its agricultural business.
  • It shares a border with Benin Republic.
  • Availability of international airport and cargo terminal.
  • Gold, mica, lithium and Quetzal Ore are key mineral resources found across several LGAs.
  • Tourism attractions: Owu waterfalls which is one of the highest in West Africa.
